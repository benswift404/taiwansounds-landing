FAQ

Is TaiwanSounds free?

Yes! You can listen for free. Donations and premium sounds may come later.

Can I download the sounds?

Currently, it’s streaming only. Offline support may come later.

Yes. All recordings are currently in Taipei. We plan to record in other cities like Taichung and Kaohsiung. Reach out if you’re interested!
